# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting differing opinions and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing for mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the edge cluster

### Unacceptable

- Trolling, insulting, derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Harassing, stalking, or publishing private information
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards. Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to **conduct@agripulse.local**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
